Recurrence Relation

We want to find the minimum cost to multiply matrices from Ai to Aj. We try splitting the chain at every possible point k.

dp[i][j] = min {
  dp[i][k] + dp[k+1][j]
  + (pi-1 × pk × pj)
}
for i ≤ k < j
  • dp[i][k]: Cost of left subchain
  • dp[k+1][j]: Cost of right subchain
  • pi-1pkpj: Cost to multiply the two resulting matrices
