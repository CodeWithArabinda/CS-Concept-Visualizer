Why Binary Search?

The Power of log(n)

In a list of 1 million items, Linear Search might check all 1,000,000. Binary Search only needs 20 checks.

The Sorted Constraint

Binary Search relies on order to determine which half of the data can be safely ignored. If it's unsorted, we can't make that assumption.
