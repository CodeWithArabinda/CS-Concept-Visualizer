What is Bubble Sort?

Bubble Sort is a simple comparison-based algorithm. It works by repeatedly stepping through the list, comparing adjacent elements and swapping them if they are in the wrong order.

Why "Bubble"?

The largest unsorted element "bubbles up" to its correct position at the end of the array in every pass.

Real-life Analogy

Sorting physical files by height on a shelf by only comparing two files at a time.
